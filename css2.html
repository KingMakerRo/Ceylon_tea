The Process of making Ceylon Tea

《stage⥬1》

1.Handpicking

This is one of the important parts of making your favourite Tea, So to do it with more relaiablity and to get that Ceylon tea standard, we use humam help. The handpickers are well trained in their task to pick only the best. At this process they pick the the bud or the two young leaves which is the reason for the best cup of Tea.

《stage⥬2》

2.Withering

Once the handpicked leaves ready for withering, They are sent to the factories where the leaves will be places in large troughs to blow hot hair to breakdown the chemicals in them. This process is done to ensure the best in the process of getting th finest tea.

《stage⥬3》

3.Rolling

The Tea leaves need to mix with its enzymes due to the early process of breaking down the chemicals.This mixing is done by usinf rollers at different size.

《stage⥬4》

4.Fermentation/Oxidisation

Once the Tea leaves exits its final roller it is spread out and left to ferment/oxidise this part of the process is cruisul because it is what adds flavour and aroma to the tea.

《stage⥬5》

5.Firing and Drying

The tea at this stage of this tea makinng process, the thea already has the color and aroma it needs.This stage is about maintaing that set standard by destroying the enzymes.

《stage⥬6》

6.Sorting and Grading

Once the long process of frying and drying is over the tea ;eaves are now sent down to the sorting and grading stage. At this stage The tea is sorted for its size into grades like Dust, Pekeo, BOP and More.

《stage⥬7》

7.Tasting and Assessing

This is the very final that of the long Tea manufacturing process. Now the experience Tasters test the tea and decide if they go to the market or not. This tea tasting takes is done by an expert individual.
